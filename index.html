Our Philosophy

Nothing leaves our counter that we wouldn't serve at our own table.

Umri began as a home kitchen experiment and stayed one on purpose. Every cake, tart, and pastry is mixed, baked, and finished by hand in small batches, timed so it reaches you at its best — never a day earlier.

We work with real butter, real cream, and real fruit. No mixes, no fillers, no shortcuts dressed up as "artisan."
